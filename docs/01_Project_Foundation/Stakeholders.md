# Stakeholders

## Overview

This document identifies the individuals and groups who interact with, benefit from, or influence the Smart NUB Campus platform. Understanding stakeholders helps ensure that the system addresses the needs of its users while supporting the project's vision and objectives.

---

# Primary Stakeholders

## Students

Verified undergraduate and postgraduate students of Northern University Bangladesh are the primary users of the platform.

### Responsibilities

- Build academic profiles.
- Share academic resources.
- Participate in discussions.
- Ask and answer questions.
- Form project and research teams.
- Build academic networks.
- Communicate with verified students.
- Contribute positively to the community.

### Benefits

- Centralized academic collaboration.
- Easier access to study resources.
- AI-assisted learning.
- Improved networking opportunities.
- Reduced dependence on distracting social media.

---

## Platform Administrators

Administrators are responsible for maintaining the quality, security, and integrity of the platform.

### Responsibilities

- Verify student identities.
- Moderate reported content.
- Manage users and permissions.
- Monitor platform health.
- Maintain community standards.

### Benefits

- Easier community management.
- Trusted academic environment.
- Organized moderation workflow.

---

# Secondary Stakeholders

## Future Faculty Members

Future versions of the platform may allow faculty members to participate in academic discussions, mentorship, or resource sharing.

### Potential Benefits

- Improved interaction with students.
- Better knowledge sharing.
- Academic guidance.

---

## Alumni

Future versions may support verified alumni who wish to mentor students, share career experiences, or collaborate on academic initiatives.

### Potential Benefits

- Mentorship opportunities.
- Professional networking.
- Knowledge transfer.

---

# Development Team

The development team is responsible for designing, implementing, testing, and maintaining Smart NUB Campus.

### Responsibilities

- Develop new features.
- Maintain system quality.
- Improve security and performance.
- Ensure scalability.
- Keep documentation up to date.

---

# Stakeholder Relationships

| Stakeholder      | Primary Interest                          |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Students         | Learning, collaboration, networking       |
| Administrators   | Security, moderation, platform management |
| Development Team | System quality and maintainability        |
| Faculty (Future) | Academic guidance and collaboration       |
| Alumni (Future)  | Mentorship and networking                 |

---

# Stakeholder Priorities

When conflicts arise, development decisions should prioritize stakeholders in the following order:

1. Verified Students
2. Platform Administrators
3. Development Team
4. Future Faculty Support
5. Future Alumni Support
