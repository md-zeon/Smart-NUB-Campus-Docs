# Project Scope

## Overview

This document defines the boundaries of Smart NUB Campus. It specifies what is included in the current version of the project and what is intentionally excluded.

Establishing a clear scope helps maintain project focus, supports effective planning, and prevents unnecessary feature expansion during development.

---

# In Scope

The first version of Smart NUB Campus will include the following capabilities.

## Student Identity and Verification

- Student registration
- Student identity verification
- Secure authentication
- User profiles
- Role-based access control
- Account management

---

## Academic Learning

- Academic resource sharing
- Resource search and discovery
- Resource categorization
- AI-assisted learning
- Study support

---

## Community

- Academic discussions
- Question and Answer platform
- Comments and interactions
- Community participation

---

## Network

- Student discovery
- Academic networking
- Connection requests
- Student profiles
- Mentorship discovery

---

## Collaboration

- Team finder
- Team recruitment
- Project collaboration
- Research collaboration
- Competition team formation

---

## Communication

- Private messaging
- Real-time notifications
- Connection-based communication

---

## Reputation

- Reputation points
- Achievement badges
- Contribution tracking
- Community recognition

---

# Out of Scope

The following features are intentionally excluded from the current version.

## University Administration

The platform is **not** intended to replace official university systems.

Examples include:

- Course registration
- Tuition payment
- Semester enrollment
- Attendance management
- Examination management
- Grade publication
- Official transcript generation

---

## Learning Management System (LMS)

Smart NUB Campus is **not** a full Learning Management System.

The platform will not provide:

- Course creation by instructors
- Assignment grading
- Classroom management
- Teacher-controlled learning environments

---

## Social Media Platform

The platform is not designed to become a general-purpose social network.

Examples include:

- Public timeline
- Entertainment feed
- Short videos
- Personal blogging
- Non-academic content sharing

---

## Employment Platform

Although networking is supported, the platform is not intended to replace professional job portals.

Examples include:

- Job marketplace
- Recruitment platform
- Company hiring portal

---

# Project Constraints

The initial version of Smart NUB Campus is limited to:

- Verified Northern University Bangladesh students.
- Academic collaboration activities.
- Web platform.
- English and Bangla language support (future enhancement).
- Features that directly support the project's vision and mission.

---

# Future Expansion

The following features may be considered after the successful completion of the initial release.

- Faculty participation
- Alumni network
- Student clubs and organizations
- University event management
- Academic opportunity board
- Internship opportunities
- Mobile application
- Research collaboration tools
- Calendar integration

These features are outside the scope of the first version and will be evaluated based on future project requirements.
