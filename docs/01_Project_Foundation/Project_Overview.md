## Overview

Smart NUB Campus (SNC) is an AI-powered academic collaboration network designed exclusively for verified students of Northern University Bangladesh (NUB). The platform provides a centralized, distraction-free environment where students can connect, collaborate, share academic resources, participate in discussions, build project teams, and receive AI-assisted learning support.

Unlike traditional social media platforms that are designed for general communication, Smart NUB Campus is purpose-built for academic collaboration and student productivity.

---

## Vision

To build a trusted academic ecosystem where every verified NUB student can learn, collaborate, and grow together.

---

## Mission

To replace fragmented academic communication with a unified platform that promotes knowledge sharing, meaningful networking, and AI-assisted learning while reducing distractions from traditional social media.

---

## Project Goals

- Create a verified academic community exclusively for NUB students.
- Centralize academic resources and knowledge sharing.
- Enable meaningful student networking and collaboration.
- Support project and research team formation.
- Integrate AI-powered learning assistance.
- Encourage positive community contributions through a reputation system.

---

## Core User Journey

A typical student journey through the platform:

1. Register and verify student identity.
2. Complete profile with academic interests and skills.
3. Search for study resources or ask the AI assistant.
4. Participate in discussions or ask questions.
5. Discover students with similar interests or expertise.
6. Connect and communicate with verified students.
7. Collaborate through study groups or project teams.
8. Share knowledge and earn reputation by contributing to the community.

## Project Scope

### In Scope

- Student verification
- Academic networking
- Resource sharing
- AI-assisted learning
- Discussions and Q&A
- Team finder
- Private messaging
- Reputation and badges

### Out of Scope (Current Version)

- University ERP replacement
- Attendance management
- Result publication
- Course registration
- Tuition payment
- Faculty administration
- Official university management systems

---

## Guiding Principles

Every feature in Smart NUB Campus should:

- Solve a real academic problem.
- Encourage learning and collaboration.
- Build meaningful academic relationships.
- Protect student privacy.
- Reward positive contributions.
- Keep students focused on academics.

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- Verified students can collaborate without relying on external social media.
- Students can easily discover academic resources and knowledgeable peers.
- Students can build project teams and communicate within the platform.
- AI provides useful learning assistance without replacing independent learning.
- The platform encourages long-term knowledge sharing through reputation and community participation.
