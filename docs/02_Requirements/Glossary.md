# Glossary

## Academic Resource

Study material shared by students, such as notes, assignments, previous questions, or presentations.

---

## AI Assistant

An AI-powered feature that supports learning by answering academic questions, explaining concepts, summarizing content, and generating study plans.

---

## Connection

A mutual academic relationship established after a connection request is accepted.

---

## Collaboration Team

A student-created group for projects, competitions, research, or other academic activities.

---

## Department Code

The first two digits of the student ID representing the student's academic program.

---

## Discussion

A topic created for academic or university-related conversations.

---

## Intake

The semester in which a student enrolled (Spring, Summer, or Fall).

---

## Network

The collection of verified student connections within Smart NUB Campus.

---

## Pending Verification

The status assigned to newly registered students before administrator approval.

---

## Profile

A student's public academic identity containing verified university information and optional personal details.

---

## Question

An academic inquiry posted by a student to receive answers from the community.

---

## Reputation

A score representing a student's positive academic contributions to the platform.

---

## Student ID

The official identification number issued by Northern University Bangladesh, used for verification and automatic extraction of academic information.

---

## Verification

The process of confirming that a registered user is a legitimate NUB student.

---

## Verified Student

A student whose identity has been approved by a platform administrator.
