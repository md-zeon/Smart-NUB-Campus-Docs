# User Personas

## Overview

This document describes representative users of Smart NUB Campus (SNC). Each persona represents a group of students with similar goals, behaviors, and challenges.

The personas help ensure that system features are designed to solve real student problems rather than hypothetical scenarios.

---

# Persona 1 — New Student

## Description

A newly admitted student who is unfamiliar with the university environment and is looking for guidance, academic resources, and opportunities to connect with peers.

## Goals

- Build connections with classmates and seniors.
- Learn how university life works.
- Find study materials.
- Ask academic questions.
- Join study groups and project teams.

## Challenges

- Does not know many students.
- Has limited access to previous study materials.
- Unsure whom to ask for academic help.
- Relies on social media where distractions reduce productivity.

## How SNC Helps

- Student networking.
- Academic resource sharing.
- AI learning assistant.
- Discussion forums.
- Question & Answer platform.

---

# Persona 2 — Active Contributor

## Description

A student who enjoys helping others by sharing notes, answering questions, mentoring juniors, and contributing to the academic community.

## Goals

- Share knowledge.
- Build an academic reputation.
- Help other students succeed.
- Expand their professional network.

## Challenges

- Contributions often become scattered across multiple social platforms.
- Helpful resources are difficult for students to discover later.
- Receives little recognition for academic contributions.

## How SNC Helps

- Resource sharing.
- Reputation system.
- Badges and recognition.
- Community discussions.
- Student networking.

---

# Persona 3 — Team Builder

## Description

A student preparing for competitions, research, hackathons, or software projects who needs teammates with complementary skills.

## Goals

- Find skilled teammates.
- Build balanced project teams.
- Connect with students from different departments.
- Collaborate efficiently.

## Challenges

- Difficult to discover students with specific skills.
- Team formation often depends on personal networks.
- Communication is fragmented across multiple platforms.

## How SNC Helps

- Team finder.
- Student search.
- Skills-based networking.
- Connection requests.
- Private messaging.

---

# Persona 4 — Busy Student

## Description

A student balancing coursework, assignments, projects, and extracurricular activities who needs quick access to reliable academic information.

## Goals

- Save time.
- Study efficiently.
- Organize academic resources.
- Receive quick explanations of difficult concepts.

## Challenges

- Academic materials are scattered across different platforms.
- Frequently distracted while searching on social media.
- Limited time before quizzes and exams.

## How SNC Helps

- AI-powered academic assistant.
- Centralized learning resources.
- Searchable academic content.
- Discussion forums.
- Personalized study support.

---

# Common Needs

Although students have different goals, all personas share several common needs.

They need:

- A trusted academic community.
- Verified student identities.
- Reliable academic resources.
- Efficient communication.
- Opportunities for collaboration.
- A distraction-free environment.
- AI-assisted learning support.

---

# Design Considerations

The platform should:

- Prioritize simplicity and ease of use.
- Encourage meaningful academic interactions.
- Minimize distractions.
- Reward positive contributions.
- Protect student privacy.
- Support collaboration across departments.
