# Context

Smart NUB Campus (SNC) is designed as a trusted academic collaboration platform exclusively for students of Northern University Bangladesh (NUB).

Students need a safe environment where they can confidently share academic resources, communicate with peers, form project teams, and collaborate without concerns about fake accounts or anonymous users.

General social media platforms do not verify academic identity, making it difficult to determine whether a user is genuinely affiliated with the university.

---

# Decision

Only verified Northern University Bangladesh students shall be granted access to Smart NUB Campus.

Every new account must complete the student verification process before accessing any protected platform features.

Verification requires:

- A valid student ID.
- An uploaded university student ID card.
- Administrative approval.

Accounts remain in the **Pending** state until approved by an administrator.

---

# Rationale

This decision establishes trust as a fundamental characteristic of the platform.

Restricting access to verified students:

- Creates a trusted academic community.
- Prevents fake or anonymous accounts.
- Encourages meaningful collaboration.
- Improves confidence when connecting with other students.
- Reduces spam and malicious behavior.
- Supports professional networking within the university.

---

# Consequences

## Positive

- Trusted community.
- Higher quality interactions.
- Reduced spam.
- Safer communication.
- Reliable networking.
- Better moderation.

## Negative

- Manual verification introduces administrative work.
- New users cannot access the platform immediately.
- Registration may take longer than public platforms.

---

# Alternatives Considered

## Open Registration

Allow anyone to register.

**Rejected because:**

- No guarantee of academic identity.
- Increased spam and fake accounts.
- Reduced trust between students.

---

## Email Verification Only

Verify users using an email address.

**Rejected because:**

- Does not guarantee the person is an active NUB student.
- Easier to misuse than identity verification.
