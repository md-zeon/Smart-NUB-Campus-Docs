# Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)

## Overview

This section documents the major architectural and product decisions made during the design of Smart NUB Campus (SNC).

Rather than only describing **what** the system does, these documents explain **why** specific design decisions were made and the consequences of those decisions.

Each decision is intended to provide historical context for future contributors and maintain consistency across the project.

---

## Purpose

The goals of Architecture Decision Records are to:

- Preserve important design decisions.
- Document the reasoning behind architectural choices.
- Improve consistency across documentation.
- Help future contributors understand the project.
- Prevent repeating the same design discussions.

---

## Decision Lifecycle

Each decision follows the same structure:

- Context
- Decision
- Rationale
- Consequences
- Alternatives Considered

---

## Decision Status

Possible statuses are:

- Proposed
- Accepted
- Deprecated
- Superseded
